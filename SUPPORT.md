## Reporting a Bug

Open a [GitHub Issue](https://github.com/Ascension-EIP/Ascension/issues/new) and include:

- A clear title and description.
- Steps to reproduce the problem.
- Expected vs. actual behaviour.
- Relevant logs or screenshots.
- The affected component (`server`, `ai`, `mobile`, or infrastructure).

---

## Security Issues

**Do not open public issues for security vulnerabilities.** See [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for the responsible disclosure process.

---

## Feature Requests

Open a [GitHub Issue](https://github.com/Ascension-EIP/Ascension/issues/new) with the `feat` label and describe the use case and expected behaviour.
